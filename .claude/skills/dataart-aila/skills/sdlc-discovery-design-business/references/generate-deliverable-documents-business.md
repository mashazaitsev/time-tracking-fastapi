# Business Deliverable Generation Skill

## Overview

Business-focused deliverable generation emphasizing stakeholder communication, business case documentation, and organizational alignment.

## Business Deliverable Types

### Strategic Documents

**Business Case Document**
- Executive summary
- Problem statement and opportunity
- Solution overview
- Business benefits and ROI
- Implementation approach
- Risk assessment
- Resource requirements
- Success metrics

**Stakeholder Communication Plan**
- Stakeholder analysis and mapping
- Communication objectives
- Key messages by audience
- Communication channels and timing
- Feedback mechanisms
- Success measures

### Operational Documents

**Change Management Plan**
- Change impact assessment
- Stakeholder readiness analysis
- Training and support strategy
- Communication approach
- Resistance management
- Success metrics

**Business Process Documentation**
- Current state process maps
- Future state process design
- Process change analysis
- Training requirements
- Performance metrics

**Governance Framework**
- Decision rights and authorities
- Escalation procedures
- Quality assurance processes
- Compliance requirements
- Monitoring and reporting

### Performance Documents

**Success Metrics Framework**
- Business KPIs and targets
- Measurement methodology
- Reporting cadence
- Dashboard design
- Baseline establishment

**Risk Management Plan**
- Risk identification and assessment
- Mitigation strategies
- Contingency planning
- Monitoring approach
- Escalation procedures

## Business Document Population Guidelines

### Information Sources

**From Requirements (clarification.md):**
- Business objectives
- Stakeholder needs
- Success criteria
- Constraints

**From Research (research/):**
- Market analysis
- Best practices
- Technology options
- Competitive landscape

**From Design (design/detailed-design.md):**
- Solution architecture
- Business process design
- Integration approach
- Performance expectations

**From Implementation Plan (implementation/plan.md):**
- Project timeline
- Resource requirements
- Risk mitigation
- Success metrics

### Business Language Guidelines

**Executive Level:**
- Focus on strategic impact
- Emphasize business value
- Include ROI and metrics
- Keep technical details minimal

**Operational Level:**
- Detail process changes
- Include training requirements
- Specify support needs
- Provide implementation timeline

**Technical Level:**
- Include technical specifications
- Detail integration requirements
- Specify performance criteria
- Provide troubleshooting guidance
